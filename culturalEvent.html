RULES:

CATEGORY : GROUP DANCE

Short Compelling Description:

Rules:

EVENT FORMAT :

Goonj shall consist of 3 rounds: Online screening, Prelims and Finals.
Participants can register in two categories:
• Desi Xtravaganza (Indian songs)
• Western Xtravaganza (English songs).

DATES:

27th October EOD : Last date for registration
30th October EOD : Last date to send audio/video for online prelims
1st November : Result of online prelims
9th November : Prelims (On-campus)
9th November : Finals (On-campus)

RULES (Prelims):

• Participants need to send their audio/video, not longer than 3 minutes, to culturalcomm@ximb.ac.in for online screening. The participants will be evaluated upon the audio/video. The qualified participants will go to the prelims.
• For prelims, each participant will be required to sing ONLY ONE song for 3 minutes.
• The participant has an option of using an instrument to complement his/her song. He/she can himself/herself play the instrument while singing or can be accompanied by someone on the instrument.
(Note: Usage of Instrument is optional however it is participant’s responsibility to make their own arrangement for their instrument as instruments will not be provided by us. No brownie points will be awarded for the instrument.)
• There will be a clear demarcation between the vocalists participating for Desi Xtravaganza and Western Xtravaganza.
• No Karaoke will be allowed in the prelims.
• Vocalists in both categories can sing any cover or original song composed by them.
• Original compositions are welcomed but that does not guarantee brownie points. It will solely depend on the performance.
• Participants applying for both categories will have to give two separate performances.
• The prelims will be held off stage.
• This round will be an elimination round. Participants qualifying in the Prelims will compete in the Finals.

RULES (Finals) :

• 10 participants will be ideally shortlisted from both the categories, i.e., Desi Xtravaganza and Western Xtravaganza. The number may change depending upon the participation in all the categories.
• Each participant will be given 5 minutes for a song in each of the two categories. Exceeding the time-limit will result in penalty in the points awarded.
• Participants qualifying in both categories will have to give two separate performances
• The participant has an option of using an instrument to complement his/her song. He/she can himself/herself play the instrument while singing or can be accompanied by someone on the instrument.
(Note: Usage of Instrument is optional however it is participant’s responsibility to make their own arrangement for their instrument as instruments will not be provided by us. No brownie points will be awarded for the instrument.)
• Participants can use Karaoke for this round.
• Vocalists in both categories can sing any cover or original song composed by them.
• Original compositions are welcomed but that does not guarantee brownie points. It will solely depend on your performance.
• Final round will be an on-stage event.
• The judges may use their discretion to allow surprise round after finals.

Short Compelling Description:

What do you see in a mirror? Also known as, pratichhaya or pratibimb - it has numerous names; call it anything, our reflection never leaves us. In it we see our innate desires, hopes, unfulfilled dreams, inner demons, burdened soul or maybe at times a free spirit. There’s a face behind each face, it could be uglier or even more beautiful than the mask one dons. It seeks to be let out. It wants to be seen. What better than painting that idea on a face?
Presenting “Pratibimb” – the face painting competition where participants get to paint their imagination on their subjects’ faces unleashing their artistic spirits.

Rules

1. This is a face painting competition.
2. The artist will have to carry their own paints and paint brushes. Costumes, wigs, accessories, gemstones, glitter, feathers, silicon or prosthetics are NOT allowed.
3. Participation will be in the form of teams, each consisting of only 2 people – The artist and the model (whose face would be painted). Both of them should be from the same institute.
4. Each team will get a maximum of an hour to paint.
5. There will be only 1 winning team and 1 runners-up team.
6. Each team will get 30 seconds to explain their concept to the judges.
7. Judging will take place immediately after the competition gets over. The scoring and decisions made are at the discretion of the judges and their decision shall be final.

Deadlines:

2. On spot registrations also allowed if total registrations for the event doesn’t cross the cut off for the total participants that we can accommodate for the event.
3. In case of any disputes or issues, the committee reserves the right to decide and settle the same.
