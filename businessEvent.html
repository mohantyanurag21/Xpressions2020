MAVEN

Event Description :

Maven is a national level case study based competition organized by CONSTRAT – The Consulting and Strategy consortium of XIMB that brings together the best brains from across the country's finest business schools.

Rules

Team Size: 8-15 + 2 CAs (for lights and sounds) compulsory
Round(s): 3 (Eliminations, Semi-Finals and Finals)
Judging Criteria: Choreography, Energy, Synchronization, Expressions, Stage utilization and stage presence, Costumes, Innovation in song incorporation and fast forward section into performance, Overall impact
Stage Specifications: Closed Auditorium with the audience on one side of the stage.

Elimination

Teams must perform any sequence of their choice on Bollywood songs.
Teams can use non-Bollywood songs for a maximum of 1 minute in their performance.
Time Limit: 3-5 minutes (+1 min setup and clearance time)

Semi-Finals:

Teams can perform an extended version of the piece performed in eliminations.
Teams must perform any sequence of their choice on Bollywood songs.
Teams can use non-Bollywood songs for a maximum of 1 minute in their performance.
Time Limit: 4-6 minutes (+1 min setup and clearance time)

Finals:

Perform an extended version of the piece performed in semi-finals.
Teams need to add a fast-forward section for at least 20 secs where they have to perform (for example 30 second) part of their choreography fast-forwarded to 1.25X or more( to make it 24 seconds or less). The song which is fast-forwarded must also be present in your track before the fast forward section.
Time Limit: 8-10 minutes (+2 minutes setup and clearance time)
Teams can use non-Bollywood songs for a maximum of 2 minutes in their performance.

General Rules:

Only one team per college is allowed.
Participants should use more than one song in their performance.
Only Hindi Bollywood tracks are allowed. No regional language or other songs allowed. (Use of any other tracks will result in a high penalty)
Usage of props is allowed. These have to be brought by the teams themselves but the organizers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage.
The losses/damage to the props is not the responsibility of organisers and will be heavily penalised
Teams are STRICTLY prohibited from mentioning their college names during the performance by any medium. Any team doing so will be awarded a high penalty.
Teams have to submit their tracks (in mp3 format only) and prod-list at desibeats@moodi.org before 15th December 2019 11:59 PM with subject “College Name | Team Leader's MI number” mentioning the exact time & duration where fast-forward has been used in the track.
Teams are requested to submit their props list prior to the competition and props have to be shown during practice slot. (Props will not be allowed if you fail to show them in practice slots)
The soundtrack should also be brought in a pen drive (in mp3 format only) during Mood Indigo.
Any kind of fluid, flame, powders, glitters, heavy or sharp objects or any material which have the possibility of tampering/damaging the stage is STRICTLY not allowed.
Vulgarity and obscenity at any point are strictly not allowed. It can lead to immediate disqualification. Songs should be chosen accordingly.
Performers have to clear the stage in the given time after their performance otherwise they will be penalised heavily by the organisers.
Violation of any of the above rules will lead to heavy penalties.
The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

Resonance

Event Description :

Resonance is a business simulation gaming event and the theme this year is bitcoin mining. The focus of the game is on the management of economic processes which involves cryptocurrency mining and exchange in the form of business transactions.
The game will be based on mathematical puzzles and will hold the player's attention by using trivia quizzes in between. The interest in the game lies in accurate simulation of real-world events using algorithms, as well as the sync of players' actions to expected consequences and outcomes.
The goal of the game is to mine and earn bitcoins and make profits by exchanging them with virtual money. The game will involve business decision making, strategizing, and problem solving skills. The team having the highest amount in their wallet will be declared as the winner.

COMPELLING EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Contemplate, strategize, implement! Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to make profits in the world of cryptocurrency. The world of bitcoin may be confusing and full of traps, but the rewards waiting at the end make it all worth the risk. Play it smart, and play big. The world belongs to those who throw out their safety nets. Jump right into Resonance, and stand a chance to win big!

Steps to register:

1. Register in Team of 3 members.
2. The team can comprise of both 1st years and 2nd years.
3. After Registration, each team has to appear for a preliminary quiz.
4. Duration of the quiz will be of 15 mins. ​

Rules of the Business Simulation Game:

1.The objective of the game is to be the richest mining team. Therefore at the end of the game, one needs to have the maximum money plus bitcoins.
2. There are 6 mathematical/business puzzles that need to be solved in order to mine the bitcoins within a span of 15 minutes after which you wouldn’t be allowed to solve the same mathematical puzzle.
3. During the game, there would be news items that would be flashed analyzing which the teams need to make a decision whether or not the price of the bitcoin is going to rise/fall and accordingly can sell/buy bitcoins.
4. After the first 5 minutes a Sudoku puzzle will be put on screen to be solved. The teams can solve and gain extra cash which will be added to their wallet.
5. The duration of each Sudoku game would be 20 mins and the team which solves it first would win the cash.
6. There will be a surprise one-time trivia quiz which would add bonus cash into the wallets.

Pitch It Abroad

Taking The World By Storm


The International Relations Committee(IRC) of Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar proudly announces that it will be hosting its flagship event, "Pitch It Abroad" during Xpressions, 2019. Owing to the massive success of previous editions of the event, we have decided to launch it on a larger scale inviting more applications and more brainstorming ideas from the best minds in the campus.

Candidates are required to register in teams of two and devise an elaborate marketing plan for a purely Indian product on a foreign country of their choice. Teams are expected to conduct a thorough research on the product, the market conditions in the country where they want to launch the product, the demographic and psychographic analysis of the country and will have to document their findings and plan of action in a PowerPoint presentation. They will then be asked to pitch their idea in front of an active audience which will include eminent marketing professors. The teams will be judged on the feasibility and freshness of the plan, market research and the 'pitch' they will make within the stipulated time.

So, If you think you have what it takes to unleash one among the myriad of India's endemic products on a global scale, this is the platform to test it out and in the process win lucrative prizes.

The Rules:


● Team Size: 2
● Each team has to select an existing product that is produced and sold only in INDIA. The product has to belong to one of following industries:
1. Food and Beverage
2. Handicrafts Industry
3. Automotive Industry

● Your objective is to make a pitch to sell the product in the international market.

Round 1:

Present your idea in form of a short write-up not exceeding 300 words at the time of Registration. The write-up should comprise the product details, the country/Place (Outside India) and reason for choosing the particular product and country/place.

Round 2:

Qualified contestants have to pitch the product with the help of PowerPoint presentation not exceeding 5 slides .
Your pitch must comprise of the following:
✓ Product name and description
✓ The country in which you wish to sell the product.
✓ The price (in the respective country currency)
✓ The target audience
✓ The marketing strategy
✓ Source of Investment and breakeven point

● The time limit for the pitch is 7 minutes followed by 3 minutes of Q/A.
● The judge you'll be pitching to will be a marketing faculty. So prepare accordingly.
● The decisions of the judge shall be final and binding.
● In case of any issues pertaining to the rules, the decision of the IRC team will be final and binding.
● The top 3 pitchers will win a complimentary stay in GOA.

The Prize:

A 3 night complimentary, luxurious stay in some of Goa's finest hotels. Each winning team will get one voucher. Each voucher offers you a double occupancy room.
