About Xpressions

Xpressions is a Management Cultural Festival organized by Xavier Institute of Management Bhubaneswar (XIMB). Xpressions is an event that plans to reach out to the best minds in business schools to test their business acumen as well as their zest for life. It is a three-day event packed with business simulation games, cultural activities, fun field games and much more. Taking a break from their hectic schedule, Xpressions gives its guests, a one of a kind experience, rejuvenating and refreshing them.

Being the biggest annual b-school festival in the whole of India, Xpressions has everything you need. A celebration that starts 100 days prior to the event, it has something in store for everyone. This year as well, Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar, invites you to be a part of an event that the entire nation will witness in all its glory.
